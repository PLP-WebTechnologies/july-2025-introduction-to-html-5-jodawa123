What is Healthy Eating?

Healthy eating means choosing foods that nourish your body — like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and proteins — while limiting added sugars and processed foods.

Simple Tips

1. Eat more fresh fruits and vegetables.

2. Drink plenty of water.

3. Limit fast food and sugary snacks.
